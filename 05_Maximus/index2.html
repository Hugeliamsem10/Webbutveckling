Information

Team Cherry is an indie game development studio based in Adelaide, South Australia, best known for creating the critically acclaimed game Hollow Knight. Founded by Ari Gibson, William Pellen, and Jack Vine, the studio gained recognition for its focus on detailed hand-drawn art, challenging gameplay, and rich atmospheric storytelling.

Their debut title, Hollow Knight (2017), quickly became a landmark in the indie gaming scene, praised for its expansive world design, fluid combat, and hauntingly beautiful soundtrack. The success of the game cemented Team Cherry’s reputation as one of the most influential modern indie studios.

Team Cherry released the sequel Hollow Knight: Silksong (2025), a highly anticipated sequel that shifts the focus to Hornet, a key character from the original game. With a devoted fanbase eagerly awaiting its release, Team Cherry continues to stand out for crafting deeply immersive experiences despite being a small, independent team.
